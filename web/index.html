  • The page builds a notes duration histogram in real time when you play on connected MIDI device.
  • You can use this tool when you want to analyze your playing style from a technical perspective: steady rhythm (histogram should not move as a whole to left or right), notes of different values having different durations (distinguishable and narrow histogram peaks corresponding to specific note values), etc.
  • Intended to work with technical exercises rather than musical pieces.

Powered by WebMidi.js and Plotly.js. Source code available on GitHub. Works in your browser, no data collected.
